# Become a Provider

Do you manage laboratories that could be leveraged by a wider community of students and researchers worldwide? Are you interested in securely sharing and possibly monetizing access to your lab equipment without complicated agreements or intermediaries? DecentraLabs makes it straightforward and secure.

Becoming a provider means joining a network of institutions that share resources online, transparently and efficiently. You list your laboratories on the DecentraLabs marketplace, set the access conditions and receive internal service-credit settlement through your institution's managed account. Service credits are not `$LAB`, ERC-20 payments or cash-redeemable balances.

The active flow uses institutional SSO for people and a verified institutional backend with a managed wallet for on-chain operations. Providers do not connect a personal wallet, choose a network or pay gas in the Marketplace. Reservations and lab access remain backed by the configured smart contracts and the Lab Gateway.

All you need to start is a functioning, self-contained laboratory, undergo a simple institutional validation, and register your lab through our easy-to-use platform. We’ll guide you through the entire process.
